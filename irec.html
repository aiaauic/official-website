UIC IREC Team

The IREC team designs, builds, tests, and launches high-power sounding rockets to compete in the Spaceport America Cup, which is an international intercollegiate rocket engineering competition. Each year, the team is tasked to develop a unique experimental payload for the competition. This annual event takes place in late June at the Spaceport America complex in Las Cruces, New Mexico.
