High Power Rocketry Certification

The program enables members to construct their own rockets and attempt the High Power Rocketry (HPR) certification. Success in the certification would offer numerous benefits, such as Level 1 certification, which grants legal permission to purchase and use H and I impulse class motors. Similarly, Level 2 certification allows the purchase and use of J, K, and L impulse class motors.
