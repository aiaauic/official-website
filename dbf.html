UIC DBF Team

The DBF team designs and builds radio-controlled aircraft to participate in the Design/Build/Fly competition, an annual international intercollegiate competition hosted by AIAA. The team's design process focuses on ensuring that the prototype aircraft meets the competition's evolving requirements and mission themes. The team is divided into two subgroups: one that specializes in structural and aerodynamic design and construction of the aircraft, while the other specializes in propulsion and electronic control systems.
