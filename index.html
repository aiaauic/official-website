Mission

Our mission is to inspire and promote student involvement in aerospace related fields within campus. We strive to provide our members with opportunities to develop technical and leadership skills through competitions, networking events, and professional development activities. Our members participate in the Intercollegiate Rocket Engineering Competition (IREC) and the Design/Build/Fly (DBF) competition. We also have a program that helps our members receive their National Association of Rocketry (NAR) High Power Rocketry (HPR) certification.

Member Benefits

1. Networking Opportunities: We provide members with the opportunity to network with professionals in aerospace related industries. This can be particularly helpful for students seeking internships or employment after graduation.

2. Conferences and Events: We host various conferences, workshops, and events throughout the year. Members have the opportunity to attend these events and gain valuable knowledge and experience.

3. Scholarships and Awards: We offer scholarships and awards to student members to recognize their achievements and provide financial support for their education.

4. Leadership Opportunities: We provide leadership opportunities for members, allowing them to develop important skills such as communication, teamwork, and problem-solving.
